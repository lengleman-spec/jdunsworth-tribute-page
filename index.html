Early Life

John Francis Dunsworth was born in Bridgewater, Nova Scotia, on April 12, 1946. He was the second of ten children and initially pursued theatre studies at the University of Guelph, before leaving university in his fourth year. Dunsworth cited a mandatory philosophy class as the reason for his drop out.

Dunsworth quickly became an influential figure in Halifax's arts scene - starring in over 25 productions at Neptune Theatre, and founding the groundbreaking Pier One Theatre in 1970. In 1987, he started Filworks Casting, becoming one of the region's top casting directors.

Personal Life

Off-screen, Dunsworth was known for his love of sailing, bridge, Scrabble, and stonework. He married Elizabeth Bates in 1974, and raised four children with her, two of whom appeared with Dunsworth on Trailer Park Boys.

Career

His most iconic role was that of Jim Lahey, the perpetually drunk trailer-park supervisor in the cult comedy Trailer Park Boys. His most iconic quotes are listed below.
